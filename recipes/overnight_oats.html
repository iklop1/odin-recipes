Recipe:

These overnight oats are a great lunch option and so easy to make the night before.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon sweetener (honey, maple syrup, stevia, etc.)
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup frozer blueberries
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/4 cup granola

Instructions:

  1. Place all ingredients (except granola) into a large container and mix until combined.
  2. Cover the container with a lid or plastic wrap. Place in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, overnight, or up to a few days.
  3. Uncover, add granola, and enjoy!
