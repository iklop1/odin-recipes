Recipe:

This easy baked muushroom rice casserole is great as a side dish for a big dinner or an easy main-dish for a quick weeknight meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1.3 lb (650 g) mushrooms, quartered
  • 3.5 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups long grain rice, uncooked
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3 shallots/green onions/scallions
  • 1-2 tablespoons butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Place mushrooms in a large bowl. Pour over hot melted butter, add garlic, salt, and pepper. Toss well.
  3. Place remaining ingredients (except shallots and extra butter) in a 9x13 baking pan.
  4. Mix and shake the pan to spread the rice evenly.
  5. Spread the mushrooms over the rice - they should cover the whole surface area.
  6. Drizzle over olive oil.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until mushrooms are golden. Remove and let rest for 5 minutes.
  8. Add extra butter and scallions. Toss through the rice. Enjoy!
